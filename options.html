FAQ

  • How do I view the notes on mobile devices?
  • Due to limitation from Google side, chrome extensions are not available on mobile devices. However, you could click the share button above text area, and view the notes from Simple Mobile CRM. The app is available for iPhone, iPad and Android, and is free.
  • Would the current extension be affected after Simple Mobile CRM integration?
  • The short answer is 'no, your existing notes, services and privacies would NOT be affected'. In case you have further concerns, please visit our CRM FAQ for the detailed answers.
  • When the note is saved?
  • By default, the note is saved whenever the text area is out of focus (e.g. click anywhere else on the page, or view another email).
  • May I get more information about the extension?
  • Please visit the https://www.bart.com.hk/simple-gmail-notes/ for the detailed instructions and policies.
  • How do I refresh the Gmail page?
  • Please left click the address bar and press 'Enter' key to do a full refresh.
  • Why I can't see the note abstract?
  • Some times the note abstract would take up to 2 seconds to appear. If the abstract does not appear after a long time, please help report a bug.
  • Why I need to log into my Google drive again while I already opened Google drive in another tab?
  • For security reasons, the extension could not get the Google drive access from your existing browser right away. You need to explicitly grant so.
  • How do I report a bug?
  • Please report your bug in the supporting site.
